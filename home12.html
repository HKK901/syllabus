Welcome to Syllabus Navigation (Grade 12)

The world has witnessed a radical change in the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in recent years, and this process is still ongoing. The relevance of computer education has been increasing day by day. Realizing this, the curriculum of computer science for Grades 11 and 12 has been developed according to the National Curriculum Framework, 2076.


This website is designed to help students navigate through the syllabus of Computer Science for Grade 11. You can click on the chapters on the left to view the content of the respective chapters. Although not as detailed as the textbooks, this website provides a brief overview of the chapters, enough to help you understand the content and get a recap.
